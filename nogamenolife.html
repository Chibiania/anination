No Game No Life

Sora and Shiro are siblings who have shut themselves in their house choosing instead to play games all day. Online, they are known as the unbeatable team Blank. One day, the god of an alternate world transports them to his world where there is no violence and everything is determined by games. There are 16 races with the human race ranked lowest and living in its last remaining city.

Suzuka

Yamato Akitsuki moves to Tokyo to study at a local high school. Living with his aunt, who runs a female only bath house, he starts his normal days at school. One day, he notices a very beautiful girl, Suzuka, at school. Yamato is delightfully surprised when he realizes she lives next door to him. Slowly, his life normal ordinary life starts to change.

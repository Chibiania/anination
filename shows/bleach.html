Bleach

Ichigo Kurosaki is not the average high schooler. He possess the ability to see ghosts and spirits. One day, a shinigami, Rukia Kuchiki, saves him and his family from a Hollow. However, she is injured and unable to continue the fight. As a result, she transfers her powers to Ichigo to finish off the Hollow. Afterwards, Ichigo and Rukia team up to defeat the rest of the Hollows in Ichigo's town.

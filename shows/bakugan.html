Bakugan Battle Brawlers

Dan Kuso along with his friends, Shun, Runo, Marucho, Julie, Shun, and Alice, form the "Bakugan Battle Brawlers". They play a game Dan and Shun made up with mysterious cards that fell from the sky. However, the gang gets dragged into a fight to save both earth and the Bakugan's home world from the rogue bakugan, Naga.
